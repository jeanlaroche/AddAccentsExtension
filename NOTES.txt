To debug without signing the extension:
open about:debugging in firefox (as an URL). Click this firefox, and load temporary add-on.
Build it before you load it and load addAccents.xpi
Reload appears to work fine in firefox.
Go to any web page, and refresh it to reload the add-on.

In thunderbird: Tools/addons and themes, click the gear icon then debug add-on.
Submit here:
https://addons.thunderbird.net/en-US/developers/
